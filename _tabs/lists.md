Movies
------

It is easier to get educated in cinema than in literature: when a usual movie can be watched under three hours, it takes at least five times more to complete a fine novel. Although, a layman always lacks the eyes of a critic, especially if the critic is none other than great Roger Ebert. His [Great Movies](https://www.rogerebert.com/great-movies) list should be your guide when choosing a fine movie to watch, which should also help you to develop your own taste for the movies shot after Roger Ebert's demise (2013).
